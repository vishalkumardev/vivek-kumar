My Workflow

01. Project Discussion

Initial consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and project goals. We'll discuss timeline, style, and deliverables.

02. Planning & Organization

Organizing footage, creating project timeline, and establishing editing framework based on requirements.

03. Initial Edit

Creating rough cut, implementing basic transitions, and establishing narrative flow of the content.

04. Enhancement

Adding effects, color grading, sound design, and motion graphics to enhance the visual appeal.

05. Review & Revisions

Client feedback integration and making necessary adjustments to perfect the final product.

06. Final Delivery

Exporting in required formats and delivering the final product with all necessary files.
